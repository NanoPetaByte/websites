Beary Best! Chinatown
Located in an old-fashioned building with a unique art design, Beary Best! Chinatown is loaded with various facilities such as a comfortable lounge, spacious dormitories and hallways, a small pantry and a small outdoor patio.
Beary Best! Kampong Glam
Found in the vibrant creative centre of Singapore, Beary Best! Kampong Glam is a unique capsule hostel located on Arab Street. The hostel has a lovely rooftop that boasts an unblocked view.
Beary Best! Lavender
Opened in 2022, amidst the Covid-19 Pandemic, Beary Best! Lavender is a three storey hostel situated in the culturally vibrant Jalan Besar Conservation Zone.
